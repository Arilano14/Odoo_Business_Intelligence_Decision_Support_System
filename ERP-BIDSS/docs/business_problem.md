# Business Problem Definition

Tujuan: Mendefinisikan pain point perusahaan.

1. **Problem 1: Inventory Overstock**
   - **Dampak:** Biaya gudang tinggi, cashflow tertahan.
2. **Problem 2: Stockout**
   - **Dampak:** Kehilangan penjualan, customer dissatisfaction.
3. **Problem 3: Demand uncertainty**
   - **Dampak:** Pembelian tidak optimal.
4. **Problem 4: Supplier performance unknown**
   - **Dampak:** Lead time tidak stabil, risiko rantai pasok.
5. **Problem 5: Decision making too slow**
   - **Dampak:** Laporan manual, manajemen kehilangan momentum.
