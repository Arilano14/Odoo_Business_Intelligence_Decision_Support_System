# KPI Definition

- **KPI 1: Inventory Turnover**
  - *Formula:* COGS / Average Inventory
- **KPI 2: Stockout Rate**
  - *Formula:* Stockout Orders / Total Orders
- **KPI 3: Order Fulfillment Rate**
  - *Formula:* Delivered Orders / Total Orders
- **KPI 4: Forecast Accuracy**
  - *Formula:* MAPE
- **KPI 5: Supplier Performance**
  - *Formula:* Weighted Score
- **KPI 6: Business Health Score**
  - *Formula:* AHP
